Learn and Practice approach

Our main focus is to make our members more and more active towards problem solving. Whether it is solving a simple java program to making a complex interface using ReactJs. It is necessary for every student to have real life exposure and that’s why we come up with various activities and events throughout the year.

Two of our teams qualified for the Smart India hackathon 2022 finals

Team Coders4Coders

With their project made it to the finals in IIT BHU

Serious dedication and endeoveour has been performed by the students of 2nd and 3rd year to crack down one of the most prestigeous hackathon of the country where the Prime Minister himself delivered his speech. Team Coders4Coders led by Mr Abhishek Kumar Rabidas with his team of highly skilled developers made the miracle possible by cracking down to the finals of SIH2022 which led them to the soil of IIT BHU to compete against the top teams all over India where they show amazing set of skills and performed miracleously and made the name of the college proud.
