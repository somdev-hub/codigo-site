Your journey with the club as a Coordinator

In your second year, if you are interested you can apply to become an official coordinator of the club. As a coordinator you shall have be responsible to manage the club with other coordinators.

Apply to become a coordinator

Your journey to become a coordinator starts by applying for the role. You are eligible to apply if you are in your second year or above irrespective of your branch. Keep in mind that applying to become an official moderator of the club requires constant hard-work and dedication apart from your own academic curriculum. After you have applied, senior coordinators will reach out to you soon and you will be evaluated for your role. In the evaluation process, you will be asked questions related to basic coding in any language, your management skills, and then your domain specific questions. After evaluation, you will be informed whether you are selected or not. You can apply by clicking here.

Select your role

If you are selected, then your task is to select your role as a coordinator. You can get an idea about coordinators’ roles here. Remember that in your beginning years, you need to work under the supervision senior moderators who will guide you about your roles and responsibility. Apart from that you can even read about the roles and responsibility of a coordinator here. After you have known about your roles and responsibility, you are required to report to senior members before every session. They will guide you further. Along with the sessions, there are also several meetings that are organized by either the secretary or the FC which you need to attend.

Organize events, manage students and make social presence

A number of events are conducted by Codigo Plataforma every year. These includes tech fest, events in techstacy and inviting guests for important sessions. For every event held by Codigo Plataforma, coordinators are responsible to organize every thing beforehand. These include organizing seminar hall for the event, arrangement of seating, snacks, projector setup and all other related chores. Before every event the secretary will inform all the coordinators and distribute teams for the management and setup. Your role is to obey instructions and organize the events with others. As an official coordinator, you are also responsible to promote the club activities on your social network.
