Club History

Codigo Plataforma was established by the Silicon Institute of technology, Sambalpur department of Computer Science and engineering on dt - 27th apr 2019 as the official coding club of the college. This led to the foundation block of a journey of a club with not more than a hundred members and twenty coordinators to make lessons of coding and software development accessible to each student of the college and beyond.
