Coordinators

We organize, coordinate and make every event and activity success through our joint effort and team work

What do we do?

The purpose of coordinators is to manage and organise each and every club activity in smooth and secure way so that no indeciplinary action take place. It is we who take classes, make competitions and organize activities for the club members for the functioning of the club. In short we make the club work the way it works.

The Secretary

The club secretary is the head of coordinators and leads the team. It is his duty to inform the higher authority regarding classes, events and activities. He is responsible for club happenings and under his leadership the team of coordinators carry out all the tasks.
